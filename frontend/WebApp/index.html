About SurgeAlert

SurgeAlert is an intelligent flood monitoring and alert system designed for Barangay Marulas, Valenzuela City. Our mission is to provide timely, accurate, and easy-to-understand flood warnings to residents through live monitoring, visual feeds, and SMS alerts.

Purpose

The system helps residents prepare for potential floods by providing live water level data, weather forecasts, and nearest evacuation center information. Early awareness helps minimize risks to life and property.

Features

  • Real-time water level monitoring with alert levels (Green, Yellow, Orange, Red)
  • Live camera feed of Tullahan River for visual monitoring
  • Weather forecast and tide prediction to anticipate flooding
  • Evacuation center map with directions
  • SMS alert subscription to receive timely flood warnings

How It Works

The system collects water level data from monitoring stations along the Tullahan River, processes it, and updates alert levels dynamically. When water levels reach a critical threshold, users subscribed to SMS alerts will receive notifications, and the system updates the website with visual indicators and action guides. Residents can use the maps to find the nearest evacuation centers during emergencies.
